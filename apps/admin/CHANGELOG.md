# Changelog - Admin

All notable changes to the Admin (store management) app.

Format based on [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/en/1.1.0/).

## [Unreleased]

### Added
- Standardized responsive breakpoints across all screens
- B2B wholesale ordering: send orders to distributors
- AI-powered invoice import (OCR) and review
- Smart reorder suggestions based on sales patterns
- Coupon management, gift cards, and special offers screens
- Employee attendance tracking and commission management
- ZATCA compliance configuration screen
- WhatsApp management and notification settings
- Media library for product images
- Delivery zones configuration for e-commerce
- Online orders management
- Loyalty program configuration
- Branch management for multi-store operations
- Driver management and assignment
- Subscription management screen
- Wallet and payment management

### Changed
- Onboarding flow refined for new store setup
- Purchase workflow: form -> detail -> receiving goods -> supplier returns
- Settings restructured: 15+ dedicated settings screens

### Fixed
- Arabic font fallback on splash screen
- Responsive breakpoint consistency

## [1.0.0] - 2026-01-25

### Added
- Initial release: 106 screens
- Dashboard with sales stats, charts, quick actions
- Product management: CRUD, categories, price lists, product form
- Purchase management: create, list, detail, receiving, returns
- Customer management: groups, ledger, analytics
- Supplier management: form, detail
- Inventory: damaged goods, alerts, expiry tracking
- Reports: sales, profit, inventory, tax, VAT, zakat, and more
- Employee profiles and role management
- Shift management: open, close
- Debt management with monthly close
- Settings: store, POS, printers, receipts, security, backup
- Sync and conflict resolution
- Activity log and audit trail
- Multi-language (7 languages) and RTL support
