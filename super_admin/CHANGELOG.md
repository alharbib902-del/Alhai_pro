### Added

## [1.0.0] - 2026-01-25

### Added
- Initial release: 52 screens
- Platform dashboard with tenant overview and revenue
- Organization (tenant) management: create, suspend, configure
- User management across all tenants
- Subscription plan management (Basic, Pro, Enterprise)
- Store management and monitoring
- Platform-wide analytics and reports
- B2B marketplace controls and fee configuration
- Feature flags and platform settings
- Audit trail and activity logs
- Multi-language (7 languages) and RTL support
