## [1.0.0] - 2026-01-25

### Added
- Initial release: 40 screens
- Store browsing and product catalog
- Online ordering with cart and checkout
- Order tracking with real-time status updates
- Delivery address management
- Customer account and profile
- Order history and reordering
- Payment methods: cash on delivery, card
- Push notifications for order updates
- Multi-language (7 languages) and RTL support
