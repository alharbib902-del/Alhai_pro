# App Description — Alhai (App Store / Play Store)

## Title
Alhai — Neighborhood Grocery Delivery

## Subtitle
Order from your nearest grocery in minutes

---

## Full Description

**Alhai** (حي) — Your app for ordering groceries and food products from the nearest store in your neighborhood, with fast delivery to your doorstep.

### Why Alhai?

**Easy Ordering**
Browse products from neighborhood stores, add them to your cart, and order with one tap. A fully Arabic-first interface designed to be simple and fast.

**Fast Delivery**
Drivers available in your area to deliver your order as quickly as possible. Track your order live from the app.

**Fresh Products**
Trusted neighborhood stores offering fresh products at competitive prices. Know exactly where your order comes from.

**Secure Payments**
Pay cash on delivery or electronically. All transactions are secured and your e-invoices comply with ZATCA (Saudi tax authority) regulations.

### Features:
- Browse neighborhood stores and available products
- Add multiple delivery addresses
- Real-time order and driver tracking
- Complete order history
- ZATCA-compliant e-invoices
- Instant notifications for order status
- Secure account with OTP verification
- Full Arabic interface
- In-app account deletion

### Requirements:
- Saudi phone number for registration
- Age: 18 years or older
- Internet connection
