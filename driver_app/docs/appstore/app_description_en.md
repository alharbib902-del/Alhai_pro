# App Description — Alhai Drivers (App Store / Play Store)

## Title
Alhai — Drivers

## Subtitle
Delivery app for drivers

---

## Full Description

**Alhai Drivers** (حي للسائقين) — A grocery delivery app designed for drivers. Accept delivery orders from neighborhood stores, deliver to customers, and earn from every delivery.

### Why Join the Alhai Team?

**Work on Your Schedule**
Accept orders whenever you want. No fixed hours — you control your schedule.

**Transparent Earnings**
Track your earnings and commissions in real-time in the app. Every delivery is accounted for.

**Easy Navigation**
Built-in maps guide you directly to the customer's address. No searching needed — the app guides you.

**Continuous Support**
Our support team is available to help you whenever you face an issue.

### Features:
- Accept nearby delivery orders
- Direct navigation to customer address
- Real-time location tracking (during delivery only)
- Complete task and earnings history
- Instant notifications for new orders
- Proof-of-delivery photo capture
- Secure registration with OTP
- Full Arabic interface

### Requirements:
- Saudi phone number
- Age: 18 years or older
- Valid Saudi driver's license
- Suitable transportation
- Internet connection
