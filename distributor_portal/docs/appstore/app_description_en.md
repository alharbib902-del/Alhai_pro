## Marketing Page Content

### Main Headline
**Expand your distribution with Alhai — one platform to manage everything**

### Description
The Alhai Distributor Portal gives you professional tools to manage your entire distribution business. From product and inventory management to ZATCA-compliant e-invoicing — everything you need in one place.

### Key Features

**Product and Inventory Management**
Add your products, set your prices, and track inventory in real-time. Your products automatically appear in the Alhai customer app.

**ZATCA-Compliant E-Invoicing**
Automatic e-invoices compliant with ZATCA Phase 2. Simplified invoices (B2C), tax invoices (B2B), and credit/debit notes.

**Comprehensive Dashboard**
Track orders, sales, and revenue in a single easy-to-use dashboard.

**Advanced Security**
Multi-factor authentication (MFA) to protect admin accounts. Data encryption and advanced protection for financial transactions.

**Works from Anywhere**
A web portal that works from any browser — no app download needed. Supports direct A4 invoice printing.

### Who Is This For?

- Grocery and supermarket owners
- Food distribution companies
- Wholesale and retail stores
- Any commercial entity in Saudi Arabia wanting to sell products through Alhai

### Requirements

- Valid Commercial Registration (CR)
- Valid VAT number
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
- Internet connection
