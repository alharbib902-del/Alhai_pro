## 💵 Financial Model

### Revenue for Platform:
```
Per Distributor:
├── Transaction fee (2%): ~600 ر.س/month
├── Featured listing: 500 ر.س/month (optional)
└── Premium tier: 1,000 ر.س/month (optional)

With 20 distributors:
├── Transaction fees: 20 × 600 = 12,000 ر.س
├── Featured (50%): 10 × 500 = 5,000 ر.س
├── Premium (20%): 4 × 1,000 = 4,000 ر.س
────────────────────────
Total: 21,000 ر.س/month from B2B
```

### Costs:
```
├── Platform infrastructure: shared
├── Support: 1 person (3,000 ر.س)
└── Marketing: 2,000 ر.س
────────────────────────
Net: 16,000 ر.س/month
```

## 🔄 Operations

### Order Flow:
```
1. Store owner browses catalog
2. Places wholesale order
3. Distributor receives notification
4. Confirms order
5. Ships goods
6. Updates delivery status
7. Auto-generates invoice
8. Receives payment
9. Platform deducts 2% fee
```

### Automation:
- ✅ Auto pricing updates
- ✅ Auto invoice generation
- ✅ Auto payment tracking
- ✅ Auto inventory sync
