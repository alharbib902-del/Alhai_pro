## 1. Introduction

Alhai ("we", "us", "our") provides point-of-sale (POS) software for retail businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our applications (Alhai Cashier, Alhai Admin, Alhai Customer App, Alhai Distributor Portal, Alhai Driver App, and related services).

By using our applications, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
- Name, email address, phone number
- Business name, VAT registration number, commercial registration
- User role (cashier, admin, driver, etc.)
- Authentication credentials (stored hashed; we never store plaintext passwords)

### 2.2 Transaction Data
- Sales transactions, receipts, invoices
- Product catalog, inventory levels
- Customer information you choose to enter
- Payment records (excluding full card data, which is handled by payment processors)
- ZATCA e-invoice records (required by Saudi Arabia law)

### 2.3 Device Information
- Device model, operating system, app version
- IP address, device identifiers
- Crash logs and diagnostic data
- Language and timezone preferences

### 2.4 Location Data
- For delivery applications (Driver App, Customer App), we collect GPS location to enable delivery routing and tracking
- Location data is only collected while the app is in use or while an active delivery is in progress
- You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

- **Supabase** (database and authentication): Stores your account and transaction data. Hosted in accordance with their privacy policy.
- **Sentry** (error reporting): Collects crash reports and error diagnostics to help us fix bugs. No personally identifiable financial data is sent.
- **ZATCA** (Saudi tax authority): Receives e-invoice data as required by Saudi Arabia e-invoicing regulations.
- **Cloud storage providers**: For optional backup and media storage.

Each third party has its own privacy policy governing its use of data.

## 4. Data Retention

- **ZATCA records:** Retained for a minimum of 6 years, as required by Saudi Arabia tax law. These records cannot be deleted before the retention period expires.
- **Account data:** Retained while your account is active. You may request deletion (see Section 6).
- **Diagnostic data:** Retained for up to 90 days, then anonymized or deleted.
- **Backups:** Retained for up to 30 days.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We only share data:
- With third-party service providers listed above, strictly to provide our service
- When required by law, court order, or government authority (including ZATCA)
- To prevent fraud, abuse, or security threats
- With your explicit consent

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Delete** your account data, except for ZATCA records that must be retained by law
- **Export** your data in a portable format
- **Withdraw consent** where processing is based on consent
- **Object** to certain processing activities

## 7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:
- Data in transit is encrypted using TLS
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
- Row-level security (RLS) enforces tenant isolation
- Access to production systems is restricted and logged
- Regular security reviews and updates

No system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and authorities as required by applicable law.

## 8. Children's Data

Our applications are intended for use by businesses and adults (18 years or older). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us and we will delete it.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in data centers located outside Saudi Arabia, subject to appropriate safeguards. Certain data categories (such as ZATCA invoices) are stored in compliance with Saudi Arabia data residency requirements where applicable.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in-app and via email where possible. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
